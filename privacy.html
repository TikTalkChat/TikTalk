Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 13, 2025

Welcome to TikTalk ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our stranger chat service (the "Service").

Information We Collect

Because our service is designed for anonymous conversations, we collect very limited information:

  • Chat Messages: Messages sent between you and a stranger are transmitted through our servers. We do not store your conversations after the chat session ends. Messages are ephemeral and are only held temporarily to ensure delivery to the other user.
  • Connection Data: We may temporarily log technical data, such as your IP address, to facilitate the connection between you and another user. This data is not stored long-term and is only used for the duration of your session to operate the service.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect any personal information like your name, email address, or phone number.

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the Service.
  • To connect you with another random user for a chat session.
  • To ensure the technical functionality of the Service.

Data Storage and Security

Your chat messages are not stored on our servers once your conversation with a stranger ends. We implement reasonable security measures to protect the data while it is being transmitted, but no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to help operate and maintain the technical functionality of our app, such as hosting and connection servers. These services only process data that is necessary for them to function and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe we might have any information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
